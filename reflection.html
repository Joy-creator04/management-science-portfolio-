Course Reflection

Reflecting on my learning journey throughout Management Science.

Most Interesting Project

The Traveling Salesman Problem was the most interesting project because it demonstrated how mathematical models can improve transportation and logistics decisions.

Most Challenging Project

Decision Tree Analysis was the most challenging because it required evaluating multiple future outcomes and understanding uncertainty in decision-making.

Most Valuable Lesson

Management Science taught me that complex decisions can be improved through structured analysis, optimization models, and data-driven reasoning.

Skills Developed

  • Optimization Modeling
  • Decision Analysis
  • Systems Thinking
  • Network Analysis
  • Spreadsheet Modeling
  • Problem Solving

Final Reflection

Throughout this course I learned how Management Science combines analytical methods and quantitative tools to improve decision-making. The activities helped me understand optimization, systems thinking, decision trees, and network analysis while demonstrating how these concepts apply to real-world business situations. These experiences strengthened my critical thinking skills and improved my ability to evaluate problems systematically.
