About This Portfolio

This portfolio documents my learning experiences in Management Science at Dong-A University. Throughout the semester I explored how mathematical models, optimization methods, systems thinking, and analytical tools can support decision-making in business and management environments.

The projects included spreadsheet optimization, systems analysis, decision trees, network routing problems, and transportation models that demonstrated real-world applications of management science concepts.

Final Reflection

Management Science taught me how structured decision-making can improve problem solving in business situations. Through optimization models, decision trees, systems thinking, and network analysis, I learned how quantitative methods help managers evaluate alternatives and identify better solutions. These activities strengthened my analytical thinking, problem-solving ability, and understanding of how data can support effective management decisions.
